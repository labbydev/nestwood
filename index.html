About Nestwood

Nestwood is a business infrastructure and project management consulting firm assisting new businesses and product developers requiring new ways of working. We understand that each project presents unique challenges that may not always fit into standard procedures or precedent. Nestwood provides the technical skills and man power to create innovative and project specific solutions for these issues. Projects and tasks that often fall into the "miscellaneous" category that are outside any existing personnel or contractors scope of work is the niche of Nestwood. Through attentive diligence, passionate dedication, and an uncompromising commitment to quality, Nestwood is eager to help you construct your vision with the tools to build your niche.

Services

consulting

Coming from a real estate operations background with a sustainability focus, we are able to offer an unique perspective to ensure maximum utility and efficiency for your clients. We also specialize in proposal formation to ensure a comprehensive and site specific proposal, allowing our clients to present an unique and tailored solution for each request.

goal setting

Nestwood assists in project management through a process of goal setting, dissection of related issues and research, and creating a project roadmap to achieve a maximum efficiency solution.

business infrastructure

Infrastructure such as roads, water, and electric are imperative for doing business in cities, the same way certain administrative and accounting components are imperative for new businesses. Creating standard procedures and policies can be challenging and take away time from production and service. Nestwood is able to step in to create those organizational procedures to keep your business organized and efficient.
