Services

Consulting

Coming from a real estate operations background with a sustainability focus, we offer an unique perspective for real estate developers. We specialize in proposal formation to ensure a comprehensive and project specific proposal, allowing our clients to present a tailored solution for each request.

Market Research

Nestwood provides market research services including: data collection, analysis and presentation. We go beyond rental and occupancy rates to include ethnographic and demographic points to provide a broader range of both quantitative and qualitative information.

Business Infrastructure

Infrastructure such as roads, water, and electric are imperative for doing business in cities, the same way certain administrative and accounting components are imperative for new businesses. Creating standard procedures and policies can be challenging and take away time from production and service. Nestwood creates basic origanizational procedures to keep your business running efficiently.

About

Nestwood is a project management consulting firm assisting new businesses and real estate developers that require new ways of working. We understand that each project presents unique challenges that may not always fit into standard procedures or precedent. Nestwood provides the technical skills and man power to create innovative and project specific solutions for these issues. Projects and tasks that often fall into the "miscellaneous" category that are outside any existing personnel or contractors scope of work is the niche of Nestwood.

Through attentive diligence, passionate dedication, and an uncompromising commitment to quality, Nestwood is eager to help you construct your vision with the tools to build your niche.
